CommentVibe

Our project develops a user-friendly website that analyzes social media comments' sentiment using machine learning and text processing techniques. Users can input a social media link and receive a breakdown of audience sentiment, enabling them to understand and respond to public opinions effectively. This technology benefits businesses and individuals by informing decisions, managing online presence, and enhancing communication. Our goal is to improve online experiences by providing valuable sentiment analysis insights.
